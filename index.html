Frequently Asked Questions

What about false positives? ↓
Zero false positives by design. We only flag vendor-documented requirements (like Stripe's idempotency) and universally dangerous patterns (like DELETE without WHERE).
Which tracers are supported? ↓
OpenTelemetry, Langfuse, Galileo, Datadog, and any JSONL trace format. We auto-detect the format.
Does it slow down CI? ↓
No. AgentCorrect scans 10,000 traces in under 100ms. It's written in Rust and runs parallel analysis.
Can I add custom rules? ↓
Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans support custom OPA/Rego policies. Define your own disaster patterns.
What languages are supported? ↓
Language-agnostic. We analyze HTTP/API traces, not source code. Works with Python, JS, Go, Java, etc.
