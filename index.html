Frequently asked questions

What trace formats do you support? ▼
We support LangSmith, LangFuse, OpenTelemetry, and custom JSONL formats. Any tool that can export agent actions as JSON will work.
How fast is the analysis? ▼
Analysis typically completes in under 100ms for 1000 traces. We use deterministic pattern matching, not ML, so it's blazing fast.
Will this slow down my CI/CD? ▼
No. AgentCorrect adds less than 1 second to your build time. Most of that is loading the traces, not the analysis itself.
What payment providers do you cover? ▼
We detect missing idempotency for Stripe, PayPal, Square, Adyen, Braintree, and 20+ other providers. New providers can be added via config.
Can I add custom rules? ▼
Yes! The Team plan includes a custom rules engine where you can define your own patterns to catch domain-specific disasters.
