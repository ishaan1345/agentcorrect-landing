The Problem

AI agents execute perfectly.
They just don't know when it's wrong.

Your AI doesn't understand business logic. It doesn't know these actions are disasters:

💳

Duplicate Payments

AI retries failed requests. Without idempotency keys, each retry creates a new charge.

stripe.charges.create() // No Idempotency-Key
🗑️

Database Wipes

AI runs DELETE queries. Without WHERE clauses, it deletes entire tables.

DELETE FROM users WHERE 1=1
🔥

Service Outages

AI clears caches. With FLUSHALL, it wipes everything and crashes your app.

redis.flushall()
The Solution

AgentCorrect understands
what your AI doesn't

It analyzes execution traces and catches dangerous patterns before deployment
