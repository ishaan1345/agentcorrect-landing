How AgentCorrect Works

Simple, automatic, and invisible to your workflow

1

Shadow Mode

AgentCorrect sits between your agent and your systems, observing all actions and corrections without interfering.

2

Learn Patterns

When operators fix mistakes, AgentCorrect automatically captures the before/after and learns the correction pattern.

3

Auto-Apply

Next time the same situation occurs, the correction is applied automatically. Operators never see the same mistake twice.
