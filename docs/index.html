Comprehensive protection

Three layers of deterministic safety checks that prevent the disasters that keep engineering teams awake at night.

💳

Payment Protection

Enforces idempotency requirements for Stripe, PayPal, Square, and 25+ payment providers. Prevents duplicate charges when your AI agents retry failed operations.

🛡️

SQL Safety Guards

Catches dangerous DELETE operations, missing WHERE clauses, and TRUNCATE commands using AST parsing. Zero false positives, 100% protection.

⚡

Infrastructure Safety

Blocks Redis FLUSHALL, MongoDB drops, and S3 bucket deletions. Prevents cache wipes and data destruction across your entire stack.
